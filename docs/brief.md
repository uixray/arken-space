# Product brief

## Goal

Run a complete two-hour custom-system tabletop session in a browser for one GM and five to six friends on a private server.

## Audience

- A GM preparing maps, characters, hidden information, music and encounters.
- Invited players who should enter quickly and control only their assigned characters and tokens.

## First-value path

1. GM opens a protected master link.
2. GM creates characters, reusable token definitions and one or more scenes.
3. GM creates reusable, revocable player access links bound to stable memberships.
4. Players join without accounts or installation and reuse the same link in later sessions.
5. GM assigns catalog skills/abilities and token controllers.
6. The group moves tokens, edits fog, draws, measures, edits sheets, chats, rolls and hears synchronized music.

## MVP boundary

Desktop web only, one campaign, orthographic 2D, square grid and a single active level. Voice/video, dynamic lighting, public registration, commerce, mobile map, offline mode, multi-level, isometric and 3D rendering are out of scope.

## Success criteria

- Preparation takes no more than 15 minutes once assets and catalog entries exist.
- Seven browser clients stay synchronized for a two-hour session.
- Server-side permissions prevent moving or editing another player's content.
- GM-layer tokens never reach player snapshots.
- Reconnect restores current state in under 10 seconds.
- Persistent state survives an application restart.
- A reusable player link never exposes its stored secret after creation or rotation.
- Full product acceptance is a 30–45 minute rehearsal with one GM and six independent player profiles after the approved backlog is implemented.
