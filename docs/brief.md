# Product brief

## Goal

Run a complete two-hour custom-system tabletop session in a browser for one GM and five to six friends on a private server.

## Audience

- A GM preparing maps, characters, hidden information, music and encounters.
- Invited players who should enter quickly and control only their own character.

## First-value path

1. GM opens a protected master link.
2. GM creates characters and one or more scenes.
3. GM generates one-time player invitations bound to characters.
4. Players join without accounts or installation.
5. The group moves tokens, reveals fog, edits sheets, chats, rolls and hears synchronized music.

## MVP boundary

Desktop web only, one campaign, orthographic 2D, square grid and a single active level. Voice/video, dynamic lighting, public registration, commerce, mobile map, offline mode, multi-level, isometric and 3D rendering are out of scope.

## Success criteria

- Preparation takes no more than 15 minutes once assets exist.
- Seven browser clients stay synchronized for a two-hour session.
- Server-side permissions prevent moving or editing another player's character.
- Reconnect restores current state in under 10 seconds.
- Persistent state survives an application restart.
